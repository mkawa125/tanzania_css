Tanzania commission for secondary schools

Background

The Tanzania Commission for Secondary Schools (TCSS) was established on 1st July 2018, under the Secondary Act (Chapter 201 of the Laws of Tanzania) to succeed the former Higher Education Accreditation Council which had been in operation since 1995. It is a body corporate mandated to recognize, approve, register and accredit Secondaries operating in Tanzania.

Our core functions

Tanzania commission for secondary schools is responsible for controlling the admission af all students completing ordinary level and passed to continue with advanced level. This was established for the main purpose to ensure that all students who has qualified to join advanced schools to be selected in the first selection without having may selections for a single entry

Mission

To provide the equality in education between private schools and government schools in Tanzania. Also to give the room for those who needs to proceed with the government schools to remove the problem of selecting students repeatedly (multiple selections) because of those who are selected to government schools but they don't report and they join private schools.
