General information about form four application 20, oct
Steps to apply
  1. Login to the system to apply for admission. to login into the system please click a login button on top of navigation bar or Click here to login. The login credentials should be as follows; username : form4 index number and password : your last name
  2. After successfully logged in student should change the password for security purpose. if you won't change the password you can not be able to apply for admission.
  3. All students who needs to continue with government schools are required to apply for admission. If the student from private schools will not apply for admission the government will admit him/her a second priority.
